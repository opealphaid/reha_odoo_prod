Installation
============

1. Navigate to **Apps**.
2. Search with keyword *viin_social*.
3. Press **Install**.

Instruction
===========

**Instruction video:** `Social Marketing <https://youtu.be/kjyus_24TDQ>`_

Access rights
-------------
   
Navigate to **Settings ‣ Users & Companies ‣ Users**, select the employee account to set up and assign corresponding rights based on job position for each individual to ensure that the individual has full rights expiration date for work. This app consists of 3 `groups of access rights <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/get-started-with-social-marketing-in-viindoo-overview-and-configuration.html#access-rights>`_ , specifically:
   
- Editor;
   
- Approve;
   
- Administrator.

Create articles on social media
-------------------------------

**Step 1:** `Create an article <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/create-articles-on-social-media.html#create-an-article>`_.

Navigate to **Social Marketing ‣ Articles ‣ Create**, fill in the article content and click **Save**.

.. image:: 01-tao-bai-viet-en.jpg
   :alt: Create articles in the Social Marketing app
   :height: 600
   :width: 1200
      
**Step 2:** `Confirm the articles <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/create-articles-on-social-media.html#article-confirmation>`_.
   
After you have checked the article content, press **Confirm** to confirm the article.

.. image:: 02-xac-nhan-bai-viet-en.jpg
   :alt: Confirm the articles in the Social Marketing app
   :height: 600
   :width: 1200

**Step 3:** `Post on social media <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/create-articles-on-social-media.html#post-confirmation>`_.
   
With the Social Marketing app, you can `connect to the social media page Facebook or LinkedIn <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/integration-social-marketing-with-social-media-page.html>`_ to be able to post and interact with viewer's comments right on the software interface without having to log in to each page. 

After you confirm the articles, the manager navigates to **Social Marketing ‣ Articles**. In the State column in the left corner of the screen, select Confirmed to filter out the posts that need to be posted, press **Post on Social Media** to confirm the post.

.. image:: 03-dang-bai-len-mang-xa-hoi-en.jpg
   :alt: Post on social media
   :height: 600
   :width: 1200

**Note**: To post on social media, you need to install the following module:
   
* `Facebook Social Marketing <https://viindoo.com/apps/app/17.0/viin_social_facebook>`_
* `LinkedIn Social Marketing <https://viindoo.com/apps/app/17.0/viin_social_linkedin>`_
   
**Step 4:** `Manage post on social media <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/manage-post-on-social-media.html>`_.

To manage all posts in the Social Marketing app, navigate to **Social Marketing ‣ Posts**.
