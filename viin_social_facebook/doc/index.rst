Installation
============

#. Navigate to **Apps**.
#. Search with keyword *viin_social_facebook*.
#. Press **Install**.

Instruction
===========

**Instruction video:** `Facebook Social Marketing <https://youtu.be/nEKGluxSGsA>`_

Concepts
--------

- Social marketing: also known as social media marketing, is a form of advertising through the creation and sharing of content on the social networks.
- Social networks: a website or an online platform, with many features that help connect people.
- Article: content need to be posted.
- Post: content are posted on social networks.

Usages
------

**1. Integrate the Social Marketing app with Facebook**

- Step 1: connect API with Facebook.

You need to access Facebook Developer to connect API.
  
  | Refer to the article about `Connect API with Facebook <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/integration-social-marketing-with-social-media-page.html#connect-api-with-facebook>`_.
  
- Step 2: connect the Facebook pages with the Social Marketing app.

Once you've finished connecting to API, you can link the specific Facebook pages you need to manage.

  | Refer to the article about `Integrate specific pages with the system. <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/integration-social-marketing-with-social-media-page.html#integrate-specific-pages-with-the-viindoo-system>`_
  
**2. Create articles on social media**

#. Step 1: create an article.
#. Step 2: confirm articles.
#. Step 3: post confirmed articles. 
#. Step 4: see the list of posts. 

  | Refer to the article about `Create articles on social media. <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/create-articles-on-social-media.html>`_

**3. Manage posts on social media**

To see interactions, users visit each post. In addition, there are several operations to manage posts such as: synchronizing posts, synchronizing pages, updating social posts, and deleting posts.

  | Refer to the article about `Manage posts on social media. <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/marketing/social-marketing/manage-post-on-social-media.html>`_

**4. Manage user interactions**

You can view all user interaction notifications in posts by navigating to **Social Marketing ‣ Notice**.
